PescaroDB Brand Design

The Brand

PescaroDB is the name of Drew Pescaro's game streaming and content creation persona. He tasked me with creating a logo based on mixing a pheonix with the symbol for shooting survivors (last gallery image), as he was injured in the UNC-Charlotte shooting in 2019. I also created a few materials for his social media and streaming (which has since moved from Twitch to YouTube) which can be seen below.
