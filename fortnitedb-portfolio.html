Fortnite DB Website

I worked with the developer of FortniteDB V2 to re-design this un-official Fortnite Database website. The goals for this re-design were to make the site easier to navigate, make the site more clear, and reduce loading times for each individual page of the site. This project was just meant to give the developer direction, with which he developed his own design using the CSS skills he has. Above is an animation of the pages I completed placed with potential transitions in an Adobe XD project, and put in an iMac mockup template. I was unfortunately limited to using a windows Google Chrome tab for the site mockup since I use a PC, but I didn't think this was a big issue.

The Home Page

The landing page was a big focus for this project, I focused on giving the user a very clear path to follow to get to what they are trying to see. This landing page will just show the top half, and have a note-card slide-up transition animation when the user scrolls down to reveal the clickable buttons below.

The Missions Page

The daily missions page will be one of the most frequented pages of the site, it will show the active 24h missions in each region of the Save the World map. I wanted to make sure people can easily identify important variables such as the power level of mission, type and location of mission, and the rewards for the mission. There are also filter tabs on the right-hand side, which was chosen to counterbalance the left-hand side pop-out menu that will be used to navigate between the 6 main pages. These filters will be determined specifically at a later point, but will allow users to find precisely what missions they are interested in instantly.

The Leaderboards Page

The database pages are very important for players seeking information about different content in a convenient location and layout, which was my focus for these pages. Users can see all of this information in the game, so I had to make this information more clear and easier to access/understand so they will use the site instead of just going through the game's UI. I focused on dividing the pages with multiple entities into a grid, with large images as links to the next page.

The Database

The leaderboards page shows off the players who are at the top of some statistic within the game, whether it be hero XP collected or the first clear of new content added in an update to the game. I made sure to highlight the top 3 players in the category to give them a sense of achievement for making it in to that spot. Currently on the site the top 3 are not highly differentiated from the rest. I continued the right-hand filter system on this page from the daily missions page above, and it will continue throughout the rest of the site.
