Pack Creative Graphics

This is a culmination of the best projects I created in my time as a graphic design intern at Pack Creative. The Women's Basketball schedule poster was a template created to be used for all the different sports schedules throughout the 2021-22 school year. I was also responsible for Women's Tennis in Spring 2021, and Wrestling in Fall 2021. I had specific brand guidelines to follow to a certain degree and worked with my bosses Chris Downey and McLean Roberts to make sure each piece is satisfactory before sending it off to the department that requested it.
