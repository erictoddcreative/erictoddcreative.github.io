This Month Page
Transformation

My goal working with Offline was to transform their MVP app into a slick, premium feeling app to accompany the 'exclusive membership' feeling they aim to give their subscribers. I focused largely on emphasizing photography/videography of their partner restaurants and slick animations to envoke that luxury feeling.

Offer Redemption
Animation

Adding in a card-swipe animation to the offer redemption process that a user takes when at the restaurant created another opportunity to show off your exclusive membership with a dark grey and gold card featuring your city's skyline filling up as it confirms your redemption.

Profile Page
Transformation

V1.0 of the Offline app had an account settings page, but there was desire to add a social element to this membership. Adding a profile where users can see their Offline history and gamify their membership leads to increased usage and less churn.
