NCSU Wellrec Marketing

These are some of my best projects from 2 years at NC State Wellness and Recreation (formerly NC State University Recreation). At this job I was in the marketing program area, so my job was to communicate with the pro-staff in the other program areas to figure out what materials they wanted for their events. These materials would be used on social media, printed as posters, and put on the tv's around the gym that rotated through advertisements. Some of the photography is my own, and some was in collaboration with other marketing student employees.
